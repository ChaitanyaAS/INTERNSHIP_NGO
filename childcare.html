Our Projects

5th Year Anniversary Celebration

The event organized at the charitable trust involved engaging activities with children, such as introductions and handicraft sessions, to foster creativity and social interaction. These activities not only allowed the children to express their artistic skills but also helped them build confidence and teamwork. The celebration of the organization's anniversary aimed at spreading joy and care among the children, offering them a day of fun and excitement.This event highlighted the importance of supporting and nurturing children’s emotional and social development.

Joy of Giving

The "Joy of Giving 2020" event was a heartfelt celebration dedicated to spreading happiness among street balloon vendor kids , ensuring they felt cared for and included during the festive season.This initiative aimed not only to light up their lives with small gestures of kindness but also to promote health and safety by providing essential protective gear. By sharing love and happiness, the event fostered a sense of togetherness and inspired the community to practice generosity and compassion.

Sportivity

The Sports Day event was a fun-filled celebration aimed at fostering teamwork, enthusiasm, and physical fitness among kids and volunteers. The day featured various engaging games and activities designed to encourage active participation and healthy competition. It provided a platform for kids to showcase their talents and for volunteers to bond with them through sportsmanship and collaboration.
