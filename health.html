"Let us remember the forgotten ones!" . It is a call to acknowledge and remember those who are often overlooked or neglected by society. It serves as a reminder to care for and show compassion towards people who may not always receive attention, whether due to their social status, age, or circumstances. The message encourages empathy and a sense of responsibility toward the well-being of others, particularly those in need or facing hardship. By reflecting on their struggles, we are reminded of our duty to uplift and support the marginalized in our communities. This sentiment calls for greater inclusivity and solidarity for those who are forgotten or left behind, ensuring no one is left out of the circle of care and support.

Blood donation is a selfless act that plays a crucial role in saving lives. This event is dedicated to acknowledging the invaluable contributions of blood donors, celebrating their generosity, and highlighting the significance of blood donation in healthcare and emergencies. It aims to inspire more individuals to participate in this life-saving act while fostering a sense of community and shared responsibility. By honoring donors, we emphasize the importance of their role in providing hope to those in need and building a healthier society.
