Children's Day Celebration

Children’s Day, also known as Bal Divas, is celebrated on the birthday of India's first Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru, who was affectionately called ‘Chacha Nehru’. This day emphasizes the importance of love, education, and affection for children, acknowledging that childhood is a time of innocence and playfulness. As Dr. Haim Ginott wisely said, "Children are like wet cement; whatever falls on them makes an impression." It is crucial to understand that every child has a unique learning style and pace, and they are all capable of succeeding when encouraged. Rather than focusing on how quickly a child learns, we should foster curiosity and a love for learning, which will last a lifetime and contribute to building a strong, educated nation. By nurturing curiosity, we are not just teaching them lessons but empowering them to continue their learning journey forever.
