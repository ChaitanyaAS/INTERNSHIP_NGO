Disaster Relief

Food Packets Distribution Drive

Hunger is something no one should struggle with or judge for! At Janmamithra Trust, in association with the Rotaract Club of Vijayanagar, we took a noble initiative to support the underprivileged. We distributed food packets, sanitizers, and masks to those in need. Additionally, we created awareness about COVID-19, emphasizing hygiene and safety practices. This effort reflected our commitment to compassion and community support during challenging times.

Distribution of Food Packets

The distribution of food packets is a thoughtful initiative aimed at addressing hunger and providing essential nourishment to those in need. It involves the preparation and delivery of wholesome meals to underserved communities, ensuring that individuals and families have access to basic sustenance. It highlights the importance of sharing resources and supporting those who face daily struggles, creating a positive impact and promoting social well-being.

Support for Balloon Vendors

The event involved distributing clothes and food packets to balloon vendors, offering support to those in need. Volunteers came together to provide essential items, ensuring the vendors had a little more comfort and nourishment. The distribution not only addressed their immediate needs but also fostered a sense of compassion and unity within the community. It was a small gesture aimed at making a positive impact and bringing some joy to the lives of those who work hard every day.
